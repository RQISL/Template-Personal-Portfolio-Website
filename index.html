Hello! I am Robert

ABOUT ME

Robert qualified with a Certificate in Computing Information Technology in 2003 and a BA Honours Degree in Multimedia in 2016. Both were from the Cork Institute of Technology. He worked as a part time ISL tutor from 2004 to 2012.The need to design materials for teaching sign language led him to pursue further studies in Multimedia. The course proved very beneficial and through the combination of his studies and experience in film he became highly skilled at product design, film making, poster generation, animation including After Effects and FCPX. He enthusiastically embraces researching digital culture and keeping abreast of new developments and opportunities in the digital age. He also became skilled in manipulating photography and web development including html, CSS, CSS3, jQuery, JavaScript, php and SQL.
